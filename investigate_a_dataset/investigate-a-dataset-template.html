Introduction¶

The dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil.We will mainly focus on whether these patients show up for their scheduled appointments or not.

Some of the questions we will be answering using this dataset include;

  1. Does neighbourhood affect patients showing up to appointments or not?
  2. Which age has the most patients showing/not showing for appointments?
  3. Does SMS_received affect patients showing up/not for their appointment?
  4. Which gender shows up for most of their appointments?
  5. When do patients schedule appointments?

Conclusions¶

In conclusion,

  1. Most patients prefer jardim camburi location
  2. Most are middle aged as seen in the findings
  3. SMS doesn't determine whether a patient shows up or not for their appointment
  4. Females are the ones that show up most for their appointments
  5. Most appointments are scheduled in 2016, with the highest month being May and most appoitments made in the morning hours

Some of the limitations with the exploration include;

  • This is just a small sample of data, a different sample might produce different findings so this can;t be conculsive
  • The data wasn't so messy however naming of columns and the way the data is entered is very hard to understand especially if one didn't collect the data and is only doing the analysis
